Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a theoretical physicist and one of the most influential scientists ever.

Timeline of Albert Einsteins life:

1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany, on March 14

1880 - His family moved to Munich

1894 - Left high school at age 15

1897 - Joined a university in Zurich and met Mileva

1900 - Graduated but was unemployed

1902 - Worked in a patent office

1903 - Married Mileva

1905 - Published special relativity

1915 - Developed general relativity

1919 - Divorced Mileva and married Elsa

1921 - Won Nobel Prize in Physics

1933 - Moved to the USA

1939 - Warned the U.S. about nuclear weapons

1955 - Einstein died
